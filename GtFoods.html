Client:

GT Foods Plus

Service

Branding and Advertising.

Redifining the essence of GT Foods Plus

GT foods Plus is a child company of GT foods, the company is based in Edo state and has been around for over 15 years. The essence of this project was to redefine GT Foods plus as a brand separate from the parent company, GT foods. Ace playhouse was tasked with branding, content creation, and ad placement.

Rebranding

The business required a new logo, so as to be easily differentiated from its parent company. Taking into account the brand's core values and essence, Ace was able to design a befitting logo.

Redesigned logo (more emphasis is placed on the "plus")

Color scheme and signifier

We added a new color which we call "smooth yellow" and the brand signifier is now the "+" symbol. It is intended for use in situations where the pluc can embody the object in focus.






Results

After designing the brands identity system, we made some applications of the logo on posters, business packages, and other brand collateral
