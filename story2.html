Redifining the essence of hair

Rayzee Signature hair is a multinational hair business with services in wig and hair production and styling. it also offers tips on hair care through its business affiliates. it currently operates in the US and in Nigeria. It had a former brand logo, but that did not fully capture the brand attribute and so had to be redesigned.

Hair and feminity

The business core audience is females, and this directed the rationale for the new logo. The redesignedlogo was influenced by feminity and universality.

Redesigned logo

Elegance is a typeface

During the redesign, we figured that the old typeface was not going to match the new logo and did not capture the brand's elegance and beauty. So we tested with a number of typefaces and Abril fatface was a great fit. It is a thin serif font with clean curves that gives a redefined touch to its elegant appearance.
