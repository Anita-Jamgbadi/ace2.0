Client:

Soso Collection

Product type:

Brand strategy, Brand identity design.

About The Brand

Soso collection is a high-end beauty brand that deals in perfumes and beauty accessories. The brand needed it's logo to resonate class, exclusivity, and ofcourse, elegance.

Design Direction

The brand identity had to feel elite. Bearing this in mind, we decided to go down the path of pure elegance, and what is more elegant than the serif typeface? The logo is a minimalist woodmark, same as the brand color.

New Logo

Results

After designing the brand identity system, we applied the logo on posters, business packages, and other brand collateral.
